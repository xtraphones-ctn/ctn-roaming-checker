Frequently Asked Questions

What is an eSIM and how does it work?
An eSIM is a digital SIM that lets you activate a mobile data plan without a physical SIM card. After purchase, you'll receive a QR code by email. Simply scan it with your phone's camera and you'll be connected to a local network at your destination.
Is my phone eSIM compatible?
Most modern smartphones support eSIM, including iPhone XS and newer, Samsung Galaxy S20 and newer, Google Pixel 3 and newer, and many other recent devices. Check your phone's settings for "Add eSIM" or "Add Cellular Plan" to confirm.
When should I activate my eSIM?
You can install the eSIM as soon as you receive the QR code (you'll need a WiFi connection). The data plan typically activates when you first connect to a network at your destination, so your validity period starts from arrival, not from installation.
Will I keep my normal phone number?
Yes! The eSIM adds a second data line to your phone. Your regular SIM stays active for calls and texts on your existing number. The eSIM handles your mobile data so you avoid expensive roaming charges.
What happens if I run out of data?
If you use all your data, simply come back and purchase another eSIM package. You can also buy a larger package before you travel if you expect heavy usage. There are no automatic top-ups or surprise charges.
