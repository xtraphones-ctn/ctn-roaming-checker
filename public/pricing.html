Frequently Asked Questions

Can I keep my existing phone numbers?

Yes, absolutely. We handle the full number porting process for you. Your numbers will transfer seamlessly to the new network with zero downtime in most cases. The switch typically completes within one working day.

What happens when my contract ends?

Our dedicated customer care team will contact you around the 18-month mark to review your usage and find the best renewal deal. We never let you roll into expensive out-of-contract rates without being contacted first.

Can I mix different plans across my business?

Yes. Each line can be on a different plan — for example, your sales team might need unlimited data while office staff only need 5GB. We'll tailor each line to actual usage to keep costs down.

Are there discounts for multiple lines?

Yes, businesses ordering 5 or more lines qualify for exclusive multi-line discounts not shown on this page. Contact us for a bespoke quote — savings of 10-25% per line are typical.

What does the annual price increase mean?

All UK mobile networks apply an annual price adjustment each April, calculated as CPI (Consumer Price Index) + 3.9%. This is an industry-standard practice. We show the exact increased price on each plan so there are no surprises.

Is EU roaming really included?

Yes, EU roaming is included at no extra cost on all plans. You can use your UK minutes, texts and data allowance in EU countries just like you would at home. Fair usage limits apply.

How long does switching take?

The entire switching process takes no more than 14 days from start to finish. Most businesses are fully switched within a week. We may also contribute towards early termination fees from your current provider — terms apply.
